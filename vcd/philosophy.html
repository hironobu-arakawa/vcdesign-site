Core of VCD (Philosophy Born from Observation)

VCD did not start from theory.
It started by observing "environments that cannot be recovered later" and separating what survived from what broke.

← Return to VCD Entrance → Japanese Version

Starting Point of Observation

VCD does not place "correct theory" first.
What it places first is observation.

Over a long time, organizations change, fields change, premises change,
yet there are things where value remained. Conversely, there are things that broke.

VCD treats this difference not as "coincidence", but as a difference in structure.

Common Points of Surviving Structures

  • Decisions are "Design Artifacts" If buried in meetings or documents, no one can explain them later.
  • Surviving structures had "Explicit Boundaries" Boundaries existed as design, not as processes or rules.
  • Responsibility must be "Traceable Later" Unless who decided, on what premise, and where remains, it will break.

What is important here is not "correctness".
It is being explainable within time.

Reason for Defining as Philosophy

Why place this as "Philosophy" rather than "Methodology"?
Because while methods can change depending on the environment, the patterns of breaking repeat surprisingly often.

VCD leaves the "premises for not breaking" in a form that does not depend on the environment.
And BOA / RP / IDG are the entry points to implement those premises as concrete boundaries.
