Manifesto — Appendix

Engineering After Vibe Coding

In the era of vibe coding,
what is the work that remains for engineers until the end?

← Back to Manifesto Read VCD

1. Vibe Coding is not the "Terminus"

Vibe coding (instant implementation by generative AI) is already becoming a prerequisite for engineering.

  • Erase coding
  • Reduce implementation cost to near zero
  • Explosively increase number of trials

What is important is the change that happens from here on.

Design is no longer "something decided carefully".
It changes to something shaped immediately as a hypothesis, and verified while breaking.

2. What the High-Speed Design Hypothesis Verification Cycle Brings

In this world, the attitude towards design is reversed.

  • Think of a correct design → ❌
  • Make everything for now and hit it against reality → ⭕

As a result,

  • Designs that look "strongest" on paper
  • Theoretically "beautiful" designs

Are born in infinite numbers. Designs given up because they cannot be made will almost cease to exist.

3. Reason for Still Not Choosing "Ultimate Design"

This is the core.
The reason it is not chosen is not technology.

The reason is responsibility.

Ultimate design maximizes the following:

  • Max guarantee
  • Max automation
  • Max optimization
  • Max coverage

But at the same time, it maximizes:

  • Max responsibility acceptance
  • Max room for misunderstanding
  • Max accident radius

So in future design, this will always be asked:

"Is that really a responsibility you should accept?"

4. When OSS Rolls into the Future, Its Nature Changes

Future OSS will not be differentiated by:

  • Convenient
  • High performance
  • Multi-functional

Because they exist infinitely.

What future OSS appeals to is not function.

  • What it guarantees
  • What it does not guarantee
  • How far it takes responsibility
  • Where it returns to humans

In other words,

"I only accept the world up to here"
A clear declaration.

5. What is Future Engineering

Engineering is no longer:

  • Making fast ❌
  • Writing correctly ❌
  • Using latest technology ❌

It is not.

The work of future engineers changes to:

  • Choosing contact points with humans
  • Deciding boundaries of responsibility
  • Deciding what not to use from countless deliverables
  • Continuing to touch the finished product without breaking it amidst high-speed feedback

6. The Question Engineers Leave at the End

In the vibe coding era,
what engineers always ask themselves is this.

"Am I making this because I *can*,
or because I can *accept* it?"

7. Conclusion in One Sentence

Future engineering is the act of
"continuing to choose only designs where responsibility can be fulfilled"
in a world where infinite things can be made.

8. Relationship with VCDesign

VCDesign is not a finished product.
Nor is it a design frame.

It is a "map of judgment"
so engineers don't get lost in the accelerated world.
